研究方向

Renewable Energy Systems

  • Solar Energy Storage Solutions: Developing next-generation materials and systems for efficient solar energy capture and long-term storage to address intermittency challenges.
  • Microgrid Optimization: Creating adaptive control algorithms for resilient community microgrids that balance multiple renewable energy sources and storage systems.

Environmental Sustainability

  • Carbon Capture Technologies: Investigating novel materials and processes for direct air capture of CO₂ and its conversion into valuable products or stable storage forms.
  • Water Treatment Innovation: Designing membrane-based filtration systems for removing emerging contaminants and recovering valuable resources from wastewater streams.
